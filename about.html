Who We Are ?

Hillside Medicare is an e-Health company offering integrated online and telephone based personal healthcare services. We are building the best network of health specialists, healthcare providers, and partners who are committed to providing health consultations by phone or online.

We are a diverse, dynamic, and dedicated team of highly committed professionals who are passionate about providing high quality, convenient access to healthcare.

The leadership team has a unique combination of healthcare, technology, and business experience across industries. As experts, they have led long and distinguished careers in top multinational organizations.

Values

1.Trust
2. Simplicity
3.Quality


We understand that the problem of convenient access to healthcare cannot be solved by any single doctor, clinic, hospital, diagnostic lab, or care provider.

Vision

Our goal is to give consumers and businesses quick, easy, and convenient access to the best health care from wherever they are, using a state-of-the-art technology platform built on a proprietary software framework. We believe that it is possible to make it easier for patients to access the best health specialists, and for healthcare providers to provide the best care to their patients. We are united by this vision. Hill Side Medicare aims to revolutionize the way healthcare is being delivered in Nigeria.
